Twitter feed plugin to display your Twitter posts on your website.

== Description ==

Want to show what's going on Twitter profile then you are in right place , Twitter Feed is the plugin to display your Tweets & photos from twitter on your site with lots of customization options.  

Twitter Feed is a WordPress plugin that displays Twitter feed of your profile or any other profile on your website. Twitter feed is very easy to use and in few minutes you can show any public Twitter posts feed on your website.

Display a completely responsive & customizable Twitter feed on your website which match with the look and feel of your website.

Twitter feed plugin supports super easy User Interface for you to easily add twitter feeds.

Twitter feed plugin have multiple themes to choose from. With complete customizabilty of colors, fonts & size your twitter feed will make your website more engaging & entertaining.

== Installation ==

The easy way :

1. Go to the Plugins Menu in WordPress.
1. Search for plugin "Add twitter".
1. Click "Install".
1. After Installation click activate to start using the twitter feed plugin.

* Go to twitter feed from Dashboard menu.
* Enable twitter feed feature.


Not so easy way : 

1. To install twitter feed via FTP
1. Download the twitter feed
1. Unarchive twitter feed plugin
1. Copy folder with add-twitter.zip
1. Open the ftp \wp-content\plugins\
1. Paste the plug-ins folder in the folder
1. Go to admin panel => open item "Plugins" => activate twitter feed.

* Go to twitter feed from Dashboard menu.
* Enable twitter feed feature.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= twitter Feed Frequently Asked Questions =

<strong>What does twitter feed plugin do ?</strong>

A. twitter feed plugin allows you to create feeds of your social media pages (Facebook, Twitter, twitter) and display them in a stream of posts. Facebook feed plugin allows design customizability to display your twitter posts beatifully. twitter feed is easy to use and require no technical knowledge to setup.

<strong>Why do I need a Feed of my social media pages (Facebok, Twitter, twitter) on my website ? </strong>

A. In today's world social media marketing is every thing, twitter is first thing most people check in the morning. In a world which is dominated by social media giants like facebook twitter & twitter you can give your visitors a view of your social media campaign on your website which if they like could result in larger social media (Facebook , Twitter & twitter) following. People follow quality.

<strong> Q. How to add new twitter Feed to your WordPress site  ? </strong>

A. After activating the twitter Feed plugin, you'll see the twitter Feed menu in WordPress menu, click on it and you'll see the Add New twitter Feed button on the top of the twitter Feeds Page.


<strong> Q. How to enable twitter Feed ? </strong>

A. To enable twitter Feed plugin, create a new twitter Feed by going to twitter Feed menu from your WordPress admin and click on the Add New twitter Feed button, under Social Media tab, you can enable or disable your desired feed.


<strong> Q. From where I get my Facebook Profile/Page ID ? </strong>

A. To get your facebook profile/page id, go in to your facebook account from any desktop browser and go into your profile, you see you facebook profile/page id from there.


<strong> Q. How to get Facebook App ID ? </strong>

A. To your facebook ID you need to create an app. Follow instruction in this tutorial to get your Facebook ID http://www.shoutmeloud.com/get-facebook-app-id-secret-key.html.


<strong> Q. How to get Facebook Secret App ID ? </strong>

A. To get your facebook secret app id. Follow instruction in this tutorial to get your Secret App ID http://www.shoutmeloud.com/get-facebook-app-id-secret-key.html 


<strong> Q. How to show limited number of feeds ? </strong>

A. You can select how many feed results should show, to do that you need to go into your previously created feeds and edit your desired feed. You'll find Results Per Feed option there.


<strong> Q. How to show/hide date of your feed ? </strong>

A. To show/hide the date for your twitter Feed, you need to go into your previously created feed, under the twitter Feed Design Settings tab you'll find the option Show Posted On Date.


<strong> Q. How to show/hide Social Media Icons of your feed ? </strong>

A. To show/hide the Social Media Icons for your twitter Feed, you need to go into your previously created feed, under the twitter Feed Design Settings tab you'll find the option Show Social Media Icon.


<strong> Q. How to show/hide Display Picture of your feed ? </strong>

A. To show/hide the Display Picture for your twitter Feed, you need to go into your previously created feed, under the twitter Feed Design Settings tab you'll find the option Show Display Picture.


<strong> Q. How to show twitter Feed in Post/Page ? </strong>

A. To show twitter Feed in your Post/Page, copy the provided shortcode into your Post where you want to show your twitter Feed.




<strong> Q. How to show twitter Feed in your Widget Area ? </strong>

A. To show twitter Feed in your Widget Area, go into your Widget area and find the Text Widget, add the Text Widget into your Sidebar area and paste the provided shortcode.




=  PREMIUM VERSION FAQs  =


<strong> Q. How to increase or decrease the twitter feed container width ? </strong>

A. To increase or decrease the width of your twitter feed container, go into your twitter Feed settings, Under the twitter Feed Settings, you'll find the option to change the width. Enter the width in pixels.


<strong> Q. How to hide the description of a feed and show only images ? </strong>

A. To hide the description of your feed, option twitter Feed settings, Under the Social Media Settings you'll find the Hide Text Content Option to show or hide the description.


<strong> Q. How to enable/disable the link to post feature ? </strong>

A. To enable/disable the link to post feature, go into your twitter Feed settings, Under the Social Media Settings tab, you'll find the Enable / Disable Links to Post option.


<strong> Q. How to change the background color of your twitter Feed ? </strong>

A. To change the background color of your twitter Feed, go into your twitter Feed settings, Under the twitter Feed Design Settings tab, you'll find the Background Color option.


<strong> Q. How to change the Text Color of your twitter Feed ? </strong>

A. To change the text color of your twitter Feed, go into your twitter Feed settings, Under the twitter Feed Design Settings tab, you'll find the Text Color option.


<strong> Q. How to change the Text Font of your twitter Feed ? </strong>

A. To change the Text Font of your twitter Feed, go into your twitter Feed settings, Under the twitter Feed Design Settings tab, you'll find the Text Font option.
